Privacy Policy for Cura Family

Effective Date: April 2026

1. Local-First Architecture

Cura Family is designed as a "Consultation Companion." We believe your health records belong to you. All Protected Health Information (PHI)—including medications, allergies, and conditions—is stored locally on your device using an encrypted database.

2. Data Collection & Use

  • No Centralized Storage: We do not operate servers that store your medical data.
  • Bare-Minimum Entry: We only require a Name/Alias to create a profile. Administrative data (DOB, Insurance) is optional and stays on your phone.
  • No Tracking: We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

3. User-Initiated Sharing

Data sharing occurs only when you explicitly initiate it (e.g., displaying a QR code to a provider or exporting a backup file). You have total control over when and with whom your data is shared.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Since we do not hold your data, we cannot delete it for you. Deleting the Cura Family app from your device permanently removes all data stored within it.
