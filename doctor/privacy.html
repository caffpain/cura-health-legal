Privacy Policy for Cura Doctor

Effective Date: April 2026

Professional Use Notice: This application is designed for healthcare providers. By using Cura Doctor, you acknowledge your role as a Data Controller under applicable health privacy laws (e.g., HIPAA, GDPR).

1. Data Ingestion & Local Storage

Cura Doctor facilitates the rapid intake of patient data via QR code scanning. Once a patient's record is scanned:

  • Data is stored locally and exclusively on the provider's authorized device.
  • Cura does not provide a centralized cloud database; we do not "see" or "process" the patient data you scan.

2. Provider Responsibility (HIPAA/GDPR)

As a medical professional, you are responsible for the security of the device running Cura Doctor. You agree to:

  • Maintain device-level encryption and biometric locks.
  • Ensure that patient data scanned into Cura Doctor is handled according to your clinical institution's data retention policies.
  • Manually delete patient records within the app once they are no longer required for active consultation or have been transcribed to a primary EHR.

3. Limited Analytics (Non-PHI)

To improve app stability, we may collect anonymous technical logs (e.g., crash reports, device model). We never collect Protected Health Information (PHI) or patient identifiers during this process.

4. No Third-Party Selling

Cura does not sell provider data or patient data. Our revenue is derived from professional subscriptions, not data monetization.

5. Data Deletion

Uninstalling Cura Doctor will result in the permanent loss of all locally stored patient intake records. Cura cannot recover this data.
